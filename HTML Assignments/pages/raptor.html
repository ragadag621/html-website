Overview

The Velociraptor is a small but fierce dinosaur that lived during the late Cretaceous period. It is known for its agility and intelligence.

Physical Characteristics

  • Length: Up to 6.8 feet (2 meters)
  • Height: Up to 1.6 feet (0.5 meters) at the hips
  • Weight: Estimated around 15-33 pounds (7-15 kg)
  • Sharp claws and teeth

Habitat

Velociraptors inhabited what is now Central Asia, particularly in areas that were once deserts and semi-arid regions.

Diet

Velociraptors were carnivores, primarily feeding on small to medium-sized dinosaurs and possibly scavenging for food as well.
